# Nonlinear Material Plugin

The purpose of this small book is to assist you in adding the material plugin with anisotropic nonlinear properties to [Lumerical](https://www.lumerical.com/).

This document is part of the "FDTD Modeling of Coherent Multiphoton Microscopy" project at the Laboratory for Optics and Biosciences at École Polytechnique (Institut Polytechnique de Paris). 

For a more in-depth exploration of multiphoton microscopy, please check out [NOLab](https://nolab.github.io/Webpage/).

Supervisor: [Dr. Nicolas Olivier](https://nolab.github.io/Webpage/)
